Initial Configuration

  1. Go to Admin tab
  2. Under COLLEGES header, click on + Add College and add your college name

    If you are a group of institutes, you can add multiple colleges here.

  3. Click on Configure icon in front of your added college name
  4. Under GENERAL header, enter required details
  5. Under DEPARTMENTS header, add your college departments
