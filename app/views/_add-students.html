Add Students

  1. Go to Students tab and click on your college name
  2. Click on the Actions button
  3. There are 3 ways to add students to the system:
    • Upload Student Database

      This is useful if you already have entire student details in an Excel.
      You can upload entire data into the system at once.

    • Invite Students to Register

      This is useful if you have student names and email IDs.
      You upload student names and Email IDs. Emails are automatically sent to students with a registration link, using which they can register online with all their details.

    • Get a Shareable Link

      If you do not have any student data, use this.
      This will generate a short registration link (e.g. iitd.tnpsuite.com). You can share this link with students by any means.
