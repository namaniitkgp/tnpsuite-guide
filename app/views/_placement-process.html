Placement Process Setup

  1. Go to Placements tab
  2. Add a new Placement Process

    Example, for final placements, you can create a process named "Campus Placement 2016-17".
    Or for MBA internships, "MBA Internships 2016-17", etc.

  3. Click on the Configure icon on the created placement process
  4. Click Edit button in the Allowed Programs section and choose programs

    Choose the programs that should be allowed to participate in this placement process.
    For example, B.Tech semester 7 and 8 students should be allowed to sit in final placements.

Placement Process

A Placement Process is the annual cycle of placements.

Placement Process is defined by its:
a). Participating colleges
b). Allowed programs (e.g. B.Tech semesters 7-8, MBA semesters 3-4)
c). Participating companies

Invitations to companies are sent out for a particular Placement Process. You can add collaborators (i.e. student coordinators, staff etc.) to a Placement Process.

Examples of Placement Process: "Campus Placements 2016-17", "Internships 2016-17"

NOTE: If you handle placements for say Engineering, MBA, Law etc. separately, you should create different placement processes for them.
